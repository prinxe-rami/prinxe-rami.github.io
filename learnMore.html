What is Eco-Art? Why is it Important?

Eco-art is art created with environmentally friendly supplies. It can be made with anything from plant-based paint to recycled boxes. The Eco-Art Revolution is important because so much in the art world is wasted. Paint is washed down drains and into water systems, while more and more trees are lost to deforestation to create wood-based art surfaces.
