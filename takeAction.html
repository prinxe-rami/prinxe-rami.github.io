How to Become an Eco-Friendly Artist

-Use organic or recycled materials that donâ€™t contain bleach as surfaces.

-When printing art, be sure to use solvent-free, nontoxic printing methods or use water-based techniques.

-For painting, try using azo or imitation pigments and plant, mineral, or water-based paints.

-To thin oil paint, use linseed or walnut oil instead of regular thinner.

-Replace spray paint with temporary chalk paint.

-Avoid glue containing Trichloroethylene and any glitter.

How Large Companies are Supporting Clean Paint

Aexcel

Aexcel creates sustainable, eco-friendly traffic marking paints. They are the first company to use soy Alkyd that doesn't compromise the permormance of the road striping paint.

Basf

The Tiexi plant of BMW Brilliance has begun using environmentally friendly paints created by BASF to coat automotives. Through this, they have helped conserve energy and the ecosystem.
